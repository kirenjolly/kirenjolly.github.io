About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to Less Crêpes Café, where every bite tells a story of French tradition and modern flair. Our journey began with a passion for bringing the authentic taste of France to the vibrant community of paris, where elegance meets convenience.

Less Crêpes Café was founded in 1986 by a group of dedicated individuals who shared a common love for exquisite cuisine and French culture. Inspired by the bustling streets of Parisian cafés, we set out to create a haven where students and locals alike could escape the ordinary and indulge in a taste of France

At Less Crêpes Café, we believe in the art of simplicity. Our philosophy revolves around crafting delicious crêpes using only the finest ingredients sourced locally, ensuring every bite is a culinary delight.
